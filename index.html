  • New Legend : Baxia
    Baxia is a shotgun user, indicating that he belongs to the short-range class. Although his shotgun's effective range is not as short as that of other old-school shooters, you can still kill someone from a distance of 7 or even 10 meters. If you reach level 15, though, you'll be able to unlock Deagle, which gives you a great medium-to-long range choice at the cost of losing the pistol's extra potential. To propel oneself into the air, fire at the ground while facing it. You may boost yourself by shooting the ground precisely opposite the direction you wish to move.
