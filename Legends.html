Adex Legends

LEGENDS

Baxia

Baxia is a shotgun user, indicating that he belongs to the short-range class. Although his shotgun's effective range is not as short as that of other old-school shooters, he is still be able to kill someone from a distance of 7 or even 10 meters.

Agger

Agger is effective at all ranges, therefore it doesn't have any negative matchups. The Assault Rifle is the Triggerman's primary weapon in the game. In addition, the Triggerman can equip supplementary weapons.

Confine

Confine's primary weapon is a Semi Auto, and he also has a backup weapon. While the current version of the weapon is still not the greatest short-range option, it no longer has a catastrophic disadvantage if your opponent closes in.

Deags

Deags has a similar look to a Runner, in that he is a man dressed in black pants and a grey shirt, yet he clearly carries his titular pistol as well. This secondary weapon may be acquired at level 15, or it can be purchased as a class.

Dimsum

Dimsum greatest advantage lies in his primary gun the M4. Dimsum can choose all types of M4 for example, M4A1, M4 MWS, and many more which has different advantage with different types of M4.


KillShot

With one of the quickest mobility speeds in the game, KillShot is geared for a fast-paced playstyle. The SMG fires at a high rate, but its rounds do not do much damage.






Pather

Pather is a rifle class that use a gun called PATHING. The PATHING fires three bursts of bullets. For a comparable playstyle, this class is sometimes likened to the Triggerman, with the main distinction being the burst function.




Samurai

Samurai may slice an adversary with their knife, which does 50 damage every slice and is a very rapid weapon. The knife's range, however, is plainly limited, and it should only be utilized in extremely close combat. Despite their great damage, this makes Samurai a challenging class to play in typical games.

Snip

The Sniper Rifle is carried by Snip. Secondary weapons are also available to the Hunter. Players must land their initial shot with this weapon due to the Hunter's low health or risk being outgunned by the other classes.






Stealth

Stealth is a high-risk, high-reward class that places a premium on precision, timing, and reflexes. Stealth is the most devastating class in the game, capable of doing 200 damage. The opposing player will always be killed no matter where the crossbow's projectile impacts on the player's body. The problem is that the Stealth class only has one ammunition and requires reloading after each shot.
